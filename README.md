# PassThePigs2

Welcome to Pass The Pigs, 2! A game where you compete with friends in an attempt to bank as many points as possible!

Pass the Pigs is a fun "dice" rolling game where the dice are cute little pigs. You roll and accumulate points and either pass, holding onto those points, or roll again, risking losing them. Once you "bank" your points you get to keep them and the first player to bank 100 points wins.

There are many ways that a pig can land, so in this Web-implemeted version, the pigs are now displayed and have a random chance of occurring depending on its point value!

This demostration allows you- the player- to go against an AI Player 3 (given that you enable them) with different strategies depending on its AI level. Level 1 is just a 50/50 on its play, level 2 attempts to play it as safe as possible, and the highest level implements greater strategy to win... Can you beat it?

Can you beat them? Find out in Pass The Pigs, 2! Now with HTML and Web implementation so you don't have to read an ugly console! (If you have time, check out Pass The Pigs (1) as a Java/Console implemented version!)
